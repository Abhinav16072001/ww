CCET(DG): 4 on-campus beverages you should be drinking
Abhinav Puri
Last updated Apr 13, 2021
1.
Coca Cola
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

When's the last time you've had a Coca Cola? It had been a while for me, but I wanted to join my friend for a non-caffeinated beverage at Bytes and I became reaquainted with the combination of carbonated water and artificial flavours. Coca cola are not so sweet as a normal soda and not so boring as a others flavored carbonated water. The Coca Cola at Bytes also have no cream in them (or maybe they do if you ask; I haven't tried), which makes it a nice drink alternative for the times when you're feeling a refreshing drink that doesn't have caffeine.

Recommendation: Coca Cola + Chips

2.
Sting
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Sting Energy Drink is a carbonated energy drink from PepsiCo International and produced by Rockstar Inc. Sting is available in three flavours, such as original Gold Rush, Gold, Power Pacq, Power Lime and Berry Blast. They have little bites to eat too, like popcorn chicken and eggrolls, in perfect snack-size portions. There's not too much seating, but there's a cute outdoor area in the back for warmer days.

String is exactly on campus and it's a short walk from there. (5 minutes total from the Gates Computer Science Building!)

Recommendation: String + an order of doritos.

3.
7 Up
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

I can't tell you whether 7 Up has a particularly amazing lime drink, only because 7 Up is the only drink I've ever ordered as lime flavour. I'm not even totally sure I know what a lemon flovoured drink is supposed to taste like. What I do know is the 7 up I ordered on a whim from here was delicious.

Recommendation: 7 Up

4.
Lipton Ice Tea
Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

When's the last time you've had a Ice tea? It had been a while for me, but I wanted to join my friend for a non-caffeinated beverage at Bytes. Iced tea is a form of cold tea. Though usually served in a glass with ice, it can refer to any tea that has been chilled or cooled. It may be sweetened with sugar, syrup and/or apple slices. It is also very refreshing.

Recommendation: Lipton Ice Tea
